Services

Monthly support includes ongoing help with your tech — answering questions, troubleshooting issues, maintaining your website, and keeping your automations running. The level of support depends on your plan (Basic, Standard, or Premium).
Basic ($100/mo) — Email support, 48-hour response, monthly check-in.

Standard ($175/mo) — Email + text support, 24-hour response, monthly check-in, website maintenance, 1 automation maintained.

Premium ($250/mo) — Email, text + phone support, same-day response, bi-weekly check-ins, all automations maintained, monthly strategy session.

Yes. Automations are included in the Standard and Full setup packages. I use tools like Zapier to connect your apps — things like automatically adding new leads to your CRM, sending follow-up emails, or syncing data between tools.

Billing & Payments

Setup packages range from $300-$800 (one-time). Monthly support ranges from $100-$250/month. Most clients start with a Standard setup ($500) and Standard monthly plan ($175/mo).
No contracts. Everything is month-to-month. I'd rather earn your business every month than lock you into something you can't get out of.
I accept credit/debit cards and ACH transfers through Stripe. You'll receive a payment link after your proposal is approved.
Setup fees are due before work begins. Monthly billing starts on the 1st of the month after your setup is complete.
You can cancel anytime with 14 days notice. No penalties, no hard feelings. I'll make sure you have everything you need to transition smoothly.

Support

Monday through Friday, 9am to 5pm Central Time. I don't offer 24/7 support — but I do respond within the timeframe promised by your plan.
Response times depend on your plan:

Basic — Within 48 hours
Standard — Within 24 hours
Premium — Same business day
Response time is how quickly I'll acknowledge your request and start looking into it. Resolution time is how long it takes to actually fix the issue — which depends on complexity. I'll always keep you updated on progress.
Monthly support covers maintenance and troubleshooting — not new builds. If you need a new website, new automations, or major changes, that's a separate project with its own scope and pricing. I'll always be clear about what's included and what's extra.
